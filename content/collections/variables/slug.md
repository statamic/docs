title: Slug
---
A slug is the string that identifies your content. It usually sits at the end of the URL.

```
<h1>{{ title }}</h1>
{{ slug }}
```

``` .language-output
<h1>My Thoughts on Bacon</h1>
my-thoughts-on-bacon
```
